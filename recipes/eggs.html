Scambled Eggs

No fuss Southern style scrambled eggs.

    Ingredients
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon butter
    Step 1
  • Whisk eggs together in a small bowl until smooth. Mix in cheddar cheese, pepper,and salt.
    • Step 2
  • Melt the butter in a skiller over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until set but still moist. Cook 3 to 5 minutes.
