Savory French Toast

French Toast taken up a notch with Parmesan.

    Ingredients
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • ground pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter ( add more as needed)
  • 4 slices whole wheat bread
    Step 1
  • Whisk eggs, Parmesan, milk and black pepper in a shallow bowl until light and fluffy
    • Step 2
  • Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
    • Step 3
  • Dip both sides of each slice of bread in the egg mixture and place in the skillet. Cook until golden brown; 2 to 3 minutes per side. Transfer to a warmed plate and dot with butter to serve.
